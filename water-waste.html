Water and Waste Water

Automated and interconnected water management systems are essential for reliable drinking water, wastewater treatment, and the protection of low-lying areas using dikes, pumps, storm barriers, and locks. Implement a robust, long-term OT cybersecurity strategy to safeguard these vital systems and ensure their continued availability and effectiveness.

Strengthening Cybersecurity for Critical Water Management Facilities:

MW Solutions has a proven track record in protecting critical water infrastructure from cyber threats globally. Our OT cybersecurity experts enhance the resilience of drinking water, wastewater, and flood control systems by assessing current setups and implementing customized governance, training, and technical controls to address industry-specific challenges.

We understand the unique security needs of the water management sector and help organizations apply established security best practices, such as IEC 62443 standards, while ensuring compliance with relevant regulations.
